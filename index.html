Event Planning

Plan It Right. Feed It Right.

You set the moment, we execute the food experience. Choose the service style that fits your event flow and guest expectations.

Buffet vs Plated

  • Buffet: Fast service, social energy, generous portions, ideal for office lunches and high-movement receptions.
  • Plated: Controlled pacing, polished presentation, strong fit for formal weddings and seated private events.
  • Both options keep the same KNUCK flavor profile and signature proteins.

Guest Count + Timeline

  • Minimum booking is 20 guests.
  • Weddings and venue-heavy events: book 6 to 12 weeks out when possible.
  • Corporate lunches and office catering: 7 to 21 days works best.
  • Final guest count target: confirm 5 days before event day.

Venue + Logistics Checklist

  • Arrival window and setup access times
  • Parking + load-in distance from kitchen/setup zone
  • Power, lighting, and table footprint for service line
  • Indoor/outdoor backup plan for weather shifts
  • On-site contact for final walk-through

Staffing + Service Options

  • Delivery and setup for ready-to-serve food
  • Buffet line management for high-volume events
  • Plated service support for formal seating plans
  • Breakdown support for clean and efficient closeout
  • Custom menu builds when your event needs more than standard packages

Policies

Clear Terms. No Surprises.

These policies keep planning clean for both sides and help us deliver strong service on event day.

Planning

Early planning gives you better menu flexibility and smoother event logistics. Share your date, venue, and expected count as soon as possible so we can hold your slot.

Availability

Dates are first-come, first-served and confirmed only after deposit. Peak wedding and holiday weekends can fill quickly.

Guest Count Confirmation

Final headcount is typically due 5 days before service unless otherwise agreed. Billing is based on the final confirmed count, with a minimum of 20 guests.

Changes to Orders

Menu and logistics changes are welcome when timing allows. Last-minute changes may affect staffing, inventory, and pricing.

Cancellations

Cancellations must be submitted in writing. Deposits secure production and date hold; partial credit or transfer may be offered at KNUCK discretion based on notice and prep status.

Pricing

Listed pricing is per person, food only. Add 20% service fee and applicable tax. Quotes remain valid for the stated approval window.

Payment Terms

A 50% deposit is required to reserve your date. Remaining balance is due before service unless other terms are approved in writing.

Delivery / Travel Fees

Delivery and setup are free in Lehi and Provo. Salt Lake City suburbs are billed +$30. Extended travel may require a custom fee.

Staffing / Service Fees

Service labor needs vary by event style and timeline. Additional staffing may be required for plated service, long service windows, or large guest counts.

Rentals / Setup / Cleanup

Standard food setup is included. Rentals such as specialty tables, linens, or extra service gear can be added as custom line items when requested.

Perishable Food / Food Safety

Food is prepared and transported under safe handling standards. Once service is complete and food is outside active control, safe holding responsibility transfers to the client/site.

Allergy Disclaimer

Tell us about allergies and dietary restrictions during planning. While we can flag and adjust menu items, cross-contact risk cannot be fully eliminated in all environments.

Substitutions / Seasonal Availability

Ingredient quality comes first. If availability shifts, KNUCK may substitute with equivalent or better options and will communicate any meaningful changes.
